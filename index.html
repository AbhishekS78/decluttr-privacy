Decluttr Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

Decluttr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, photos, or files to any server. All photo processing, duplicate detection, and screenshot analysis happens entirely on your device. We never see your photos or any data about them.

Gallery Access

Decluttr requires access to your photo gallery to show, organize, and delete photos. This permission is used only to display photos within the app and to delete photos you explicitly select. We do not read, copy, or upload your photos anywhere.

Analytics and Advertising

Decluttr shows banner and occasional full-screen advertisements to free users provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect non-personal usage data as described in Google's privacy policy. Pro subscribers see no advertisements of any kind. We do not use any other analytics or tracking services.

Subscriptions

Decluttr Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information. Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds are handled through your Google Play account.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Decluttr may send weekly reminders to clean your gallery. These are scheduled locally on your device. You can disable notifications anytime in your Android Settings.

Data Stored Locally

Decluttr stores the following data locally on your device only: cleanup statistics, app preferences, and screenshot category results. This data never leaves your device and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support.shadowforge@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
